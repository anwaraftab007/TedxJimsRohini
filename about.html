About TED

TED is a non-profit organisation devoted to Ideas Worth Spreading. The annual TED Conference invites the world's leading thinkers and doers to speak for 18 minutes.

Their talks are then made available, free of charge, on TED.com.

TEDx is a grassroots initiative that brings the spirit of TED to local communities around the globe through TEDx events. These events are organized by passionate individuals who seek to uncover new ideas and to share the latest research in their local areas that spark conversations in their communities. TEDx events include live speakers and recorded TED Talks, and are organized independently under a free license granted by TED.

ABOUT TEDX JIMSROHINI


TEDxJIMS Rohini is here to create a unique gathering in our community that will release new ideas, inspire, and enlighten under the theme, Seeds of Transformation: Cultivating Minds, Connecting Worlds.

The event will showcase thought-provoking talks from diverse speakers who will explore how small ideas can spark meaningful change, foster personal and societal growth, and bridge connections across cultures, disciplines, and experiences.

With a curated lineup of speakers, interactive sessions, and opportunities for networking, TEDxJIMS Rohini is designed to ignite curiosity, challenge perspectives, and inspire action among attendees, creating a ripple effect of transformation both locally and globally.
